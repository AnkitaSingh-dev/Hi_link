About HI-LINK

We all aspire and work hard to buy our own homes - where we feel loved, safe and happy. We want our families to enjoy the security of that home, and celebrate auspicious and joyful occasions there. Yet, we wonder - when will this dream come true? The HI-LINK group has invested the many years in making this wish a reality for over families. It now invites you to fulfil yours. Spanning many projects across Indore , the dedicated team has developed many urban and countryside landscapes. The company believes in keeping its commitment to providing budget-friendly housing while ensuring that it delivers the quality, standards, and excellence it has been renowned for. Today, the HI-LINK has gone beyond housing and has to its credit properties like Banquet Halls, Luxury Resorts, Water Parks, Excavations, City clubs and more. Having built their goodwill over two and a half decades, the group is ensuring that a beautiful home is everyone's right and within everyone's reach.
